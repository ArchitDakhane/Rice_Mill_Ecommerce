Kolam Rice

Kolam rice is another exquisite variety, cherished for its premium quality and fine texture. Cultivated in the fertile fields of Maharashtra, it offers a delightful combination of taste and aroma. Grown under ideal conditions of 20-25°C (68-77°F) and a relative humidity of 50-65%, Kolam rice stands out as a versatile and favored choice for a wide range of culinary delights.
